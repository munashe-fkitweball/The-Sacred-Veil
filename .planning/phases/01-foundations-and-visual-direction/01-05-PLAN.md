Purpose: Without these repairs, every atlas link to one of these 6 places would render as a dead `#orphan-id` anchor (per `app.js:78` orphan-fallback behavior). The atlas would ship broken. This plan removes that failure before it happens.

Output: Modifications to `data.js`, `data_sections_*.js`, `tooltips.js`, and `page_configs.js` per the audit. Each repair is small and targeted; the plan is one-task-per-orphan to keep the changes auditable.
